Ryanair 2024

The Great Peel Deal

Agency
Tropical Minds London
Summary
Flights so sweet, they're bananas
The Challenge

Ryanair faced declining brand perception as consumers increasingly viewed budget airlines as commoditized and joyless. The brand needed a disruptive cultural moment to transform negative associations into playful engagement while maintaining their low-cost positioning.

The Insight

People don't just buy flights—they buy stories they can share, and nothing travels faster than a smile.

"Nothing travels faster than a smile"
The Idea

We transformed Ryanair's entire fleet into flying bananas using premium Chiquita-inspired branding, creating the world's first fruit-themed airline experience. This audacious brand partnership turned every flight into a shareable moment of pure joy, making budget travel unexpectedly delightful.
