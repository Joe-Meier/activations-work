Urban Essence Collection

Agency

Summary
Beauty in the unexpected
The Challenge

Chanel needed to disrupt the traditional floral fragrance market and speak to a younger generation increasingly concerned with urban authenticity. The brand risked being perceived as out of touch with modern metropolitan realities.

The Insight

Today's consumers crave raw, unfiltered experiences that reflect their lived urban reality rather than escapist fantasy.

"Beauty exists in the spaces we actually inhabit"
The Idea

We created five limited-edition fragrances capturing the olfactory DNA of global megacities—from Beijing's industrial complexity to Mumbai's street-food vibrancy. Each scent was molecularly engineered to evoke authentic urban atmospheres while maintaining Chanel's luxury positioning.

The Results

The campaign exceeded every benchmark. Then broke them.
